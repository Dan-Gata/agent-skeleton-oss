**Add Instruction:**
```
User Click "➕ Instruction"
    ↓
Modal Opens
    ↓
User Fills Form
    ↓
User Submit
    ↓
POST /api/instructions/add
    ↓
ConversationMemory.addInstruction()
    ↓
SQLite INSERT
    ↓
Response Success
    ↓
Alert "✅ Instruction ajoutée"
    ↓
loadInstructions() Refresh
    ↓
New Instruction Appears
```
